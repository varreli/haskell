Pythagorean Triples:


Note primative pythagorean triples have no common divisors

i.e. the entries are relatively prime.


Only two sides including the hypotenuse can be prime :

(3, 4, 5)
(5, 12, 13)

2 cannot be a side of a triple. (see mod 8)
